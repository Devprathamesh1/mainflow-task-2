BMW X5

The BMW X5 is a mid-sized luxury SUV that combines power and elegance. It comes with various engine options including a turbocharged inline-six and a plug-in hybrid variant. The X5 also boasts advanced safety features, a luxurious interior, and cutting-edge technology.

Price: Starting from $60,000
Engine: 3.0L I6 Turbo
Horsepower: 335 hp
Top Speed: 150 mph
