BMW 7 Series

The BMW 7 Series redefines luxury and performance in the flagship sedan category. With its premium materials, advanced features, and superior engine options, the 7 Series is designed for those who seek refinement in every detail. The plug-in hybrid option adds an eco-friendly choice without compromising on power.

Price: Starting from $87,000
Engine: 3.0L I6 Turbo
Horsepower: 335 hp
Top Speed: 155 mph
